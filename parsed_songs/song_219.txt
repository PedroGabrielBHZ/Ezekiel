Transliteration:
V'erastich Li L'Olam
V'erastich Li B'Tzedek
U'vMishpat U'vChesed U'vRachamim
V'erastich Li B'Emunah
V'Yada'at et Adonai

Hebrew:
וְאֵרַשְׂתִּיךְ לִי לְעוֹלָם וְאֵרַשְׂתִּיךְ לִי בְּצֶדֶק וּבְמִשְׁפָּט וּבְחֶסֶד וּבְרַחַמִים. וְאֵרַשְׂתִּיךְ לִי בֶּאֶמוּנָה וְיָדַעַתּ אֶת יְהוָֹה.

Translation:
And I will betroth thee unto Me forever; yea, I will betroth thee unto Me in righteous​ness, and in justice, and in lovingkin​dness, and in compassio​n.
And I will betroth thee unto Me in faithfuln​ess; and thou shalt know the Lord.
