Ki lecha tov l'hodot ule'shimcha na'eh le'zameir, ki me'olam v'ad olam atah el.

Hebrew:
כִּי לְךָ טוֹב לְהוֹדוֹת. וּלְשִׁמְךָ נָּאֶה לְזַמֵּר כִּי מֵעוֹלָם וְעַד עוֹלָם אַתָּה אֵל.

Translation:
For it is good to thank You, and your name is pleasant to sing about, because forever and ever are You God.
