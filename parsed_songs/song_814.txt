Transliteration:
Hishbati etchem b'not Y'rushalayim, im timtz'u et dodi, ma tagidu lo?
She'cholat ahavah ani.

Hebrew:
הִשְׁבַּעְתִּי אֶתְכֶם בְּנוֹת יְרוּשָׁלָםִ, אִם תִּמְצְאוּ אֶת דּוֹדִי 
מַה תַּגִּידוּ לוֹ? 
שֶׁחוֹלַת אַהֲבָה אָנִי.

Translation:
I have made you swear, daughters​ of Jerusalem​, if you find my beloved, what will you tell him? That I am lovesick.​ (User-con​tributed translati​on)
