Transliteration:
Lo ira meriv'vot 'am asher saviv saviv shatu alai. Kuma Hashem hoshienu.

Hebrew:
לֹא אִירָא מֵרִבְבוֹת עָם אֲשֶׁר סָבִיב סָבִיב שָׁתוּ עָלַי. קוּמָה ה׳ הוֹשִׁיעֵנִי.

Translation:
I will not fear the multitude​s that have set themselve​s against me. Arise, O Lord! Save me. 
(B'ko​l Echad, pg 89)
