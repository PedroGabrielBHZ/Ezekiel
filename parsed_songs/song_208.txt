For a girl:
Y'simech Elohim
k-Sarah, Rivka, Rachel, v-Leah
 
For a boy:
Y'simcha Elohim 
k'Ephraim v'chi'Menashe
 
Yivarech'cha Adonai v'yishmerecha.
Ya'er Adonai panav eilecha v'y'chuneka.
Yisa Adonai panav eilecha v'yasem l'cha shalom.
