Transliteration:
Halelu et Hashem kol goyim, shabechuhu kol ha'umim. Ki gavar aleinu chasdo ve'emet Hashem le'olam. Halelu-ka.

Hebrew:
הַלְלוּ אֶת ה', כָּל-גּוֹיִם, שַׁבְּחוּהוּ כָּל-הָאֻמִּים. כִּי גָבַר עָלֵינוּ חַסְדּוֹ וֶאֱמֶת-ה' לְעוֹלָם; הַלְלוּ-קָהּ.

Translation:
O praise the LORD, all ye nations; laud Him, all ye peoples.
For His mercy is great toward us; and the truth of the LORD endureth for ever. Halleluja​h.
 
Psalm​s 117:1-2
