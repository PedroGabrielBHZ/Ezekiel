Transliteration:
Romemu Hashem Eloheinu, V'hishtachavu l'har kodsho.

Hebrew:
רוֹמְמוּ, יְהוָה אֱלֹהֵינוּ, וְהִשְׁתַּחֲווּ, לְהַר קָדְשׁוֹ.

Translation:
Exalt ye the LORD our God, and worship at His holy hill; for the LORD our God is holy.
