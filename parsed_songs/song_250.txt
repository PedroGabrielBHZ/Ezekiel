Transliteration:
Yasis alayich elohayich, kimsos chatan al kalah.

Hebrew:
יָשִׂישׂ עָלַיִךְ אֱלֹהַיִךְ כִּמְשׂוֹשׂ חָתָן עַל כַּלָּה.

Translation:
Thy God shall rejoice over thee, as a bridegroo​​m rejoiceth​​ over his bride.
 
Trans​lation from The Standard Prayer book by Simeon Singer (1915) (public domain)
