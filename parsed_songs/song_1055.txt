Transliteration:
Nafshi Nafshi Chamdah, chamdah bitzel yadecha,
Lidat kol raz sadecha.
 
Yeirev, yeirev na sichi, sichi eilecha,
ki nafshi ta-arog elecha.

Hebrew:
נפשי חמדה בצל ידיך,
לדעת כל רז סודיך.
 
יערב נא שיחי עליך,
כי נפשי תערוג אליך.

Translation:
My soul yearns for the shadow of Your hands,
To know each and every one of Your secrets.
 
My words for You are pleasant,​
For my soul yearns for You.
