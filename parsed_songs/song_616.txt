Transliteration:
Torat Hashem temimah meshivat nafesh; 'edut Hashem neemanah mechkimat peti.

Hebrew:
תּוֹרַת ה' תְּמִימָה, מְשִׁיבַת נָפֶשׁ;    עֵדוּת ה' נֶאֱמָנָה, מַחְכִּימַת פֶּתִי.

Translation:
God's instructi​on is pure, it revives the soul; God's testimony​ is faithful,​ it makes wise the simple.
