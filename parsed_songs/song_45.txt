Transliteration:
Omar Latzafon- teni,
Ulteiman- al tichali!
Havi'i bani m'rachok uvnotai miktze ha'aretz

Hebrew:
אֹמַר לַצָּפוֹן תֵּנִי, וּלְתֵימָן אַל-תִּכְלָאִי; הָבִיאִי בָנַי מֵרָחוֹק, וּבְנוֹתַי מִקְצֵה הָאָרֶץ

Translation:
I shall say to the North- Give them to me,
and to Yemen- set them free!
Bring​ my sons from afar, and my daughters​ from the end of the earth.
