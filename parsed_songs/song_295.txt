Transliteration:
V'hashev kohanim leavodatam, velevi'im leshiram ulezimram, ve'hashev yisrael linveihem.

Hebrew:
והשב כהנים לעבודתם, ולויים לשירם ולזמרם, והשב ישראל לנוויהם.

Translation:
Restore the priests to their service, the Levites to their song and psalmody,​ and Israel to their habitatio​ns.
 
Trans​lation from The Standard Prayer book by Simeon Singer (1915) (public domain)
