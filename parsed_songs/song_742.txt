Transliteration:


Hebrew:
ה׳ יִמְלֹךְ לְעֹלָם וָעֶד.
וְנֶאֱמַר: וְהָיָה ה׳ לְמֶלֶךְ עַל כָּל הָאָרֶץ, בַּיּוֹם הַהוּא יִהְיֶה ה׳ אֶחָד וּשְׁמוֹ אֶחָד.

Translation:
Hashem shall reign for ever and ever.
And it is said: Then will Hashem be King over all the world, on that day shall Hashem be One and his Name be One.
