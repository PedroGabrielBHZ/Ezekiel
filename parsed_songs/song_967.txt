Hebrew:
כי לה' המלוכה 
ומושל בגויים
 
והיה ה' למלך
על כל הארץ

Translation:
For the kingdom is the Lord’s,​ and he is ruler over the nations.
And the Lord shall be king over all the earth: in that day shall the Lord be One, and His name One.
