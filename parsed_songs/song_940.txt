Oz v'hadar l'vushah, vatischak l'yom acharon.

עוֹז וְהָדָר לְבוּשָׁהּ, וַתִּשׂחַק לְיוֹם אַחֲרוֹן

Translation:
Strength and dignity are her clothing;​​ and she laugheth at the time to come.
