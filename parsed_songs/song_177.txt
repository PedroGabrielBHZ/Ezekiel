Transliteration:
Eli atah v'odeka, elohai aromemeka.  Hodu l'adonai (lashem) ki tov, ki l'olam chasdo.

Hebrew:
אֵלִי אַתָּה וְאודֶךָּ. אֱלהַי אֲרומְמֶךָּ.
 
הודוּ לה' כִּי טוב. כִּי לְעולָם חַסְדּו.

Translation:
You are my God, and I will thank you, my Lord and I will praise you.  Give thanks to God for He is good, his kindness is everlasti​ng.
