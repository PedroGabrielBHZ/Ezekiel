Transliteration:
Va’ani T'filati L'cha Adonai Et Ratzon 
Elohim B’rov Chasdecha Aneini Be'emet Yishecha

Hebrew:
וַאֲנִי תְפִלָּתִי לְךָ ה' עֵת רָצון אֱלהִים בְּרָב חַסְדֶּךָ עֲנֵנִי בֶּאֱמֶת יִשְׁעֶךָ

Translation:
As for me, may my prayer come to You, O Lord, at a favorable​ moment. O God, in Your abundant faithfuln​ess, answer me with Your sure deliveran​ce.
