Transliteration:
Omdot hayu ragleinu b'shearayich, Yerushalayim. Yerushalayim Habenuyah k’ir shechubra la yachdav. 
 
L'shana haba'a b'irushalaim.

Hebrew:
עֹמְדוֹת הָיוּ רַגְלֵינוּ בִּשְׁעָרַיִךְ יְרוּשָׁלָ‍ִם.
יְרוּשָׁלַם הַבְּנוּיָה כְּעִיר שֶׁחֻבְּרָה־לָּהּ יַחְדָּו.
 
 לְשָׁנָה הַבָּאָה בִּירוּשָׁלָיִם.

Translation:
Our feet are standing within thy gates, O Jerusalem​​;
Jerus​alem​, that art builded as a city that is compact together.​
 
Psalm​ 122:2-3, translati​on from Jewish Publicati​on Society, 1917 (Open source)
