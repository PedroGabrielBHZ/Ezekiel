Transliteration:
Harachaman hu yishlach lanu et Eliyahu hanavi zachur latov, 
vivaser lanu besorot tovot, yeshuot, venehamot.

Hebrew:
הָרַחֲמָן, הוּא יִשְׁלַח לָנוּ אֶת אֵלִיָּהוּ הַנָּבִיא זָכוּר לַטּוֹב וִיבַשֶּׂר לָנוּ בְּשׂוֹרוֹת טוֹבוֹת יְשׁוּעוֹת וְנֶחָמוֹת.

Translation:
May the All-merci​ful send us Elijah the prophet, let him be remembere​d for good, who shall give us good tidings, salvation​ and consolati​on.
 
Trans​lation from The Standard Prayer book by Simeon Singer (1915) (public domain)
