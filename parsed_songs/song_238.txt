Transliteration:
Ata Vechartanu Mikol Haamim Ahavta Otanu Veratzita Vanu Veromamtanu Micol Haleshonot (2x)
 
Vekidashtanu B'mitzvotecha V'keravtanu Malkeinu LaAvodatecha Veshimcha Hagadol Vehakadosh Aleinu Karata.

Hebrew:
אתה בְחַרתָנו מכָל הַעַמִים
אָהַבְתָ אותָנו וְרַצִיתָ בָנו
וְרומַמְתָנו מכֹל הַלְשונות
ְוִקדַשתָנו במִצותֶיך
וְקֵרַבתָנו מַלְכֵנו לעַבודָתֶךָ
וְשִמךָ הַגָדול והַקָדוש
 עָלֵינו קָראתָ.

Translation:
You have chosen us from all peoples; you have loved us and taken pleasure in us, and have exalted us above all tongues; you have sanctifie​d us by your commandme​nts, and brought us near unto your service, O our King, and have called us by your great and holy name.
 
Trans​lation based on The Standard Prayer book by Simeon Singer (1915) (public domain)
