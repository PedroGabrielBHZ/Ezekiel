Transliteration:
Yamim al yemei melech toseef, shnotav kemo dor va-dor

Hebrew:
יָמִים עַל יְמֵי מֶלֶךְ תּוֹסִיף, שְׁנוֹתָיו כְּמוֹ דֺר וָדֺר.

Translation:
Mayest Thou add days unto the king's days! May his years be as many generatio​ns!
