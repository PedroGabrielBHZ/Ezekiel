Transliteration:
Baruch atah Adonai
Eloheinu melech ha'olam, asher kid’shanu b’mitzvotav v’tzivanu l’hitateif batzitzit.

Hebrew:
בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ אֱלֹהֵֽינוּ מֶֽלֶךְ הָעוֹלָם אֲשֶׁר קִדְּשָֽׁנוּ בְּמִצְוֹתָיו, וְצִוָּֽנוּ לְהִתְעַטֵּף בַּצִּיצִת.

Translation:
Blessed are you, Adonai our God,
Sover​eign of all,
who hallows us with mitzvot,
comma​nding us to wrap ourselves​ in the fringes.
