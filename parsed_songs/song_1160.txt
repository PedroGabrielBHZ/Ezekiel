Transliteration:
Ki Karov, eilecha, hadavar me-od, befichach, ubilvavcha la'asoso.

Hebrew:
כִּי קָרוֹב אֵלֶיךָ הַדָּבָר מְאֹד בְּפִיךָ וּבִלְבָבְךָ לַעֲשֹׂתוֹ.

Translation:
Because the matter is very close to you (the Torah) in your mouth and in your heart to do it.
